About Us

Strinesdale reservoir is the origin for our water hence the name of our company. It's been a privately owned reservoir that's ran in our family for centuries and as a result I felt it best that we provide the public with better than great quality water. Our filtration system is one of the best money can buy and none of the high mineral content gets flushed away when we transfer this to our bottles.
